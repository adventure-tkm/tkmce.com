Electronics and Communication

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering, started in the year 1977, has carved a niche for itself by offering the most competent instructional programme to the students. T K M College of Engineering is one of the pioneers in introducing the Bachelor Program for Electronics and Communication Engineering in Kerala. At present, B.Tech Degree course has the sanctioned intake of 60. Students from international community such as Tanzania, Nepal have successfully completed their graduation from this Department. The department has been consistently producing illustrious graduates who occupy prestigious positions in Industrial, Academic, Defense and Research fields in India and abroad. The department also offers M.Tech programme in Communication Systems from the year 2012 onwards. The sanctioned intake is 18. The department is on the verge of getting itself approved as a research centre of the University of Kerala. It has signed MoU with many leading Industrial and research organisations such as Tata Elxsi, Tata Consultancy Services, Network Systems Private Limited(NEST) etc. to carry out research, consultancy works and internships. The department has well experienced faculty with postgraduate and doctoral qualifications from prestigious institutions with many international and national publications to their credit. The faculty members are regularly updating their skills by attending and organizing refresher courses in their areas of specializations. The performance of our students in University examinations, Indian Engineering Services, GATE, CAT and such other competitive examinations are commendable. Most of the students get placement in reputed industries via campus recruitment. Our students regularly figure among the rank holders of the University. They also participate in intercollegiate competitions and used to present papers in national and international conferences. The department has well equipped laboratories and computing facility for conducting existing courses. In addition to the Central library, there is a department library with around 3000 volumes of books. Moreover it has a good collection of journals, technical magazines project reports and seminar reports. The ECE association of the department organizes various technical programmes related to academic study and personality development including national seminars, paper presentation and workshops in the state of the art technologies.

Vision

Impart quality education in Electronics and Communication Engineering through excellent teaching and research ambience, keeping abreast of the changes in technology and environment.

MISSION

  • Offer UG and PG programs at par with the curricula offered by institutions of national importance.
  • Provide robust academic, research and co – curricular ambience for moulding graduates to high quality professionals in practice and higher education.
  • Prepare graduates adaptable to the changing requirements of the society, through lifelong learning coupled with an ethical outlook and dedication.
