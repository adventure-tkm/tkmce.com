Programmes Offered

The Institution is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, and all the programmes offered in the college are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education. At present the college admits students for various graduate and post-graduate programmes as detailed below. Students are also admitted to different Ph. D programmes.
